Matthew J. Kopfensteiner

Managing Director

Matt currently works in the M&A group at TransUnion, a leading credit bureau and risk solutions provider. At TransUnion, Matt helps evaluate inorganic growth opportunities including acquisitions and minority investments. Previously, Matt was an Associate at Ansley Capital Group, a boutique healthcare investment bank where he was responsible for all aspects of the M&A process. Prior to Ansley, Matt served as a strategic and financial planning analyst at Discover Financial Services. At Discover, he developed performance metrics, monthly forecasts, and annual plans for the company’s credit card business unit.

Matt is currently an MBA Candidate at the Kellogg School of Management at Northwestern University. Matt has earned an MS in Finance from DePaul University and a BS in Management, with a concentration in Finance, from Purdue University.

He has passed Level II of the CFA exam and holds FINRA Series 63 and 79 licenses.

In his free time, Matt enjoys along Lake Michigan , playing guitar , traveling to warm climates , and learning how to cook .
